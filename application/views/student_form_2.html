INSTRUCTIONAL RESPONSIBILITIES

  1. Does your instructor speak with a modulated voice, clear diction, pronunciation and articulation?




  2. Does your instructor show effective non-verbal communication like facial expressions and gestures?




  3. Does your instructor use badly chosen words such as foul languages, etc.




  4. How do you rate your instructor's knowledge of the subject matter?




  5. Is your instructor updated with new developments in his/her area of expertise?




  6. Does your instructor relate his/her subject to other areas of discipline?




  7. Does your instructor respond reasonably to discipline problems?




  8. Does your instructor clearly set appropriate instructions and expectations to the class, and firmly implements such instructions?




  9. How would you describe your classroom set-up?




  10. Are the materials used in class organized and available?




  11. Does your instructor check the attendance and punctuality of the students?




  12. Does your instructor maintain classroom discipline?




  13. Does your instructor use his/her time in class productively?




  14. Does your instructor come to class prepared and ready to teach?




  15. Does your instructor implement instructional goals suitable to your learning needs?




  16. Does your instructor emphasize the connection of the topics being discussed to your personal improvement, future profession, and your life as a whole?




  17. Does your instructor use technology-based resources (e.g. PowerPoint presentations, etc.) in the classroom?




  18. Can you easily approach your instructor for questions and clarifications about your course?




  19. Does your instructor use other appropriate instructional materials aside from the prescribed reference materials?




  20. Does your instructor adjust his/her teaching approaches depending on your and your classmates varying needs and abilities?




  21. Does your instructor explain the different theories and concepts of the course clearly?




  22. Does your instructor ask logical, purposeful, and thought-provoking questions?




  23. How would you rate your instructor's ability to encourage you and your classmates to participate in classroom discussions? (e.g. by asking mentally stimulating questions)




  24. Does your instructor give practical and challenging assignments? If yes, was he/she able to record it?




  25. How would you rate your instructor's effectiveness in facilitating quizzes and examinations?




  26. Does your instructor return your corrected examinations, quizzes, exercises, case studies, researches, etc. on time?




  27. Does your instructor give appropriate feed back to students, who commit mistakes, without embarrassing them?




  28. Does your instructor provide intervention/remediation/consultation to at-risk students?
